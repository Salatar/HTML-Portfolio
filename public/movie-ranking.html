Salatar's Most Enjoyable Film Experiences This Year!!

Top 3 Movies...by Salatar

Dune 2

After struggling to stay awake during the first film, the Dune series really proved to be an extremely interesting and entertaining series with this addition, I can not wait for the final installment!!

Mad Max: Furiosa

An amazing and entertaining origin story for Furiosa, I have a new found love and appreciation for the Mad Max saga!!

The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug

Its been a while but a rewatch confirmed the second installment of the Hobbit trilogy was incredible. I just love the scene where the dwarves are escaping the Elven kingdom in the barrels down the river being chased by orcs!!!
